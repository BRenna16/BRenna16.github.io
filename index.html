Homepage Overview

Web browsers are one of the most important tools in modern computing because they allow users to access, search, and interact with the World Wide Web. This website introduces the history of browsers, explains how they work, and looks at where they may be headed in the future.

Commonly Used Browsers

Brief Abstract

Browsers started as very simple tools for opening linked documents, but over time they became advanced platforms that support images, video, web apps, cloud tools, and developer features. Competition between companies such as Microsoft, Google, Apple, and Mozilla helped shape the web experience people use today.

This site is based on my research from Assignment 1 and is organized into major sections for history, modern functions, future trends, key concepts, and references.
